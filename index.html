ABOUT US

Who am i?

ABC Printing Company opened for business in 1963 as ABC Business Forms, Inc. Our founder, William Strauss, began with business documents, labels and some color work. Current owners RJ and Steve Strauss, the founders 2 sons, took over our company in 1983. ABC Business Forms, Inc (as it was then known) was originally located at 5481 N Milwaukee Ave., Chicago IL. The company later moved to 5778 N Elston Ave and was there for nearly 30 years before moving to 5654 N Elston Ave.
